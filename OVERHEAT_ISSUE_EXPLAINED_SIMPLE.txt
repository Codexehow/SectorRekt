===============================================================================
                 OVERHEAT ISSUE - SIMPLE EXPLANATION
===============================================================================

WHAT WAS WRONG:
  You click right-mouse to generate CPU. The CPU bar goes up to 97-99%.
  But the Overheat bar never goes above 0% no matter how many times you click.

WHY IT WAS BROKEN:
  
  Think of it like a water fountain:
  - You turn the pump ON (right-click) = water flows UP
  - The pump automatically DRAINS water slowly (CPU decay) = water goes DOWN
  
  The game was waiting for the tank to be EXACTLY full (100.0) before
  turning on the overheat alarm. But because water is constantly draining,
  the tank can NEVER be exactly full - it only ever gets to 97-99% full.
  
  So the alarm (overheat) NEVER turns on.

THE FIX:
  
  Instead of waiting for the tank to be EXACTLY full (100.0),
  we changed the alarm to trigger when the tank is NEARLY full (95.0).
  
  Now when you click with CPU at 97-99%, the alarm turns on!

WHAT YOU'LL SEE NOW:
  
  1. Right-click repeatedly
  2. CPU bar rises and stays around 97-99%
  3. Overheat bar RISES FROM YELLOW TOWARD RED
  4. After ~20 clicks at high CPU, overheat reaches 100% and you die
  
  This is WORKING CORRECTLY now!

THE CODE CHANGE:
  
  OLD CODE:
    "if player CPU is >= 100.0, generate heat"
    Problem: CPU never reaches 100.0, so this NEVER happens
  
  NEW CODE:
    "if player CPU is >= 95.0, generate heat"
    Solution: CPU regularly reaches 95-99%, so this ALWAYS happens

WHY THIS IS GOOD FOR THE GAME:
  
  • Prevents spam-clicking (right-click spam)
  • Forces player to think strategically about CPU usage
  • Makes combat more tactical
  • Game becomes more challenging and fun
