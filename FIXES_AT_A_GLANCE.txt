PROBLEM
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
When overheat reaches 100%, nothing happens:
  ❌ Game doesn't pause
  ❌ Popup doesn't appear
  ❌ Consequence never applies
  ❌ Player is confused

ROOT CAUSE: 4 Critical Bugs in Signal Architecture
  1. Signal connection not verified (could fail silently)
  2. Signal emits 60+ times/second instead of once
  3. Popup can't receive input while game is paused
  4. No diagnostic visibility into failures
