GAMEPLAY EXAMPLE
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

TYPICAL SESSION:

1. Press Q              → CPU goes to 25
   Weapon starts charging (50% = 12.5 charge)

2. Press Q again        → CPU goes to 50
   Weapon charge increases (now ~25)

3. Left-click to fire   → Weapon fires!
   Costs 30 weapon charge
   Player can immediately generate more CPU

4. Press Q twice more   → CPU goes to 100
   All systems charging rapidly

5. When CPU hits 100    → Blink bar reaches ~25-30

6. Continue clicking Q  → All bars fill together

7. Press B              → If blink is 100, teleport!
   Costs 100 cycles
   Can now generate more CPU

8. Game continues...    → Cycle repeats
