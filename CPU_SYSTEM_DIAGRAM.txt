================================================================================
                         TANK MOVEMENT SYSTEM
================================================================================

    CONTROLS:
    - A/D or Arrow Keys: TURN
    - W/S or Up/Down: SPEED CONTROL
    - 1-5: SPEED PRESETS
    - Mouse: WEAPON AIM

    SPEED BONUS: +10% from MOVEMENT_ALLOC when CPU is being generated

================================================================================
                         CORRUPTED MODE TOGGLE
================================================================================

    KEY: G
    EFFECT: Toggle is_corrupted boolean
    SIGNAL: hallucination_triggered emitted

================================================================================
                        SIGNALS FOR UI INTEGRATION
================================================================================

    cpu_updated(current: float, weapon: float, shield: float, blink: float)
    ├─ Emitted every frame
    ├─ current: CPU pool level (0-100)
    ├─ weapon: Weapon charge (0-100)
    ├─ shield: Shield charge (0-100)
    └─ blink: Blink drive charge (0-100)

================================================================================
                          CHARGE REQUIREMENTS
================================================================================

    WEAPON FIRE:     Min 30 cycles → Cost 30 cycles → Fire projectile
    BLINK DRIVE:     Min 100 cycles → Cost 100 cycles → Teleport + reset

================================================================================
                           GAMEPLAY FLOW LOOP
================================================================================

    1. PLAYER HOLDS Q/RMB
       ↓
    2. CPU GENERATES (35/sec, max 100)
       ↓
    3. AUTOMATIC DISTRIBUTION TO ALL SYSTEMS
       ├─→ Weapon charge increases
       ├─→ Shield charge increases
       ├─→ Movement speed +10% bonus
       ├─→ Life support tracked
       └─→ Blink drive charges
       ↓
    4. SIGNAL cpu_updated EMITTED (every frame)
       ├─→ UI updates weapon bar
       ├─→ UI updates shield bar
       ├─→ UI updates blink bar
       └─→ UI updates CPU bar
       ↓
    5. PLAYER CAN:
       ├─→ Click LMB to fire (if weapon ≥ 30)
       ├─→ Press B to blink (if blink = 100)
       └─→ Continue turning & moving
       ↓
    6. PLAYER RELEASES Q/RMB
       ↓
    7. CPU DECAYS (15/sec, min 0)
       ↓
    8. CYCLE REPEATS
