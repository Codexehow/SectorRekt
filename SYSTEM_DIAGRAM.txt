================================================================================
                            KEY STATISTICS
================================================================================

VARIABLES:
  current_shield: 0-100 (actual health)
  max_shield: 100 (constant)
  current_hull: 0-100 (actual health)
  max_hull: 100 (constant)
  shield_damage_timer: 0-∞ (increments each frame)
  shield_regen_delay: 3.0 seconds
  shield_regen_rate: 20.0 points/second

CALCULATIONS:
  Shield_absorb = min(damage, current_shield)
  Remaining_damage = damage - shield_absorb
  Regen_amount = shield_regen_rate * delta_time
  Regen_active = (shield_damage_timer >= shield_regen_delay)

TIME FACTORS:
  Shield Regen Startup: 3 seconds (after last damage)
  Shield Full Regen: ~5 seconds (100 points ÷ 20 pts/sec)
  Impact Effect: 0.15 seconds
  Game Over: 2 second delay before reload
