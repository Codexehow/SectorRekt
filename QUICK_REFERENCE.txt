GAMEPLAY LOOP
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

1. Click Q or RMB               → +25 CPU
   ↓
2. CPU distributes to all systems
   ├─ 50% → Weapon
   ├─ 30% → Shield  
   ├─ 10% → Movement bonus
   └─ 10% → Blink
   ↓
3. Weapon builds up (50% means faster)
   ↓
4. When weapon ≥ 30: Left-click to fire
   ↓
5. CPU decays when idle (-15/sec)
   ↓
6. Generate more CPU to sustain abilities
   ↓
7. For Blink: Build to 100, press B

KEY MECHANICS
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

CPU GENERATION: Click-based (not holding)
  • Q Key click: +25 cycles
  • RMB click: +25 cycles
  • Max 100 cycles
  • Decay: -15/sec when idle
  • No decay while generating

CPU DISTRIBUTION: Automatic
  • 50% → Weapon (fast to charge)
  • 30% → Shield (medium)
  • 10% → Movement (slow)
  • 10% → Blink (slow)
  • All happen simultaneously

WEAPON: Thunderbolt
  • Requirement: ≥30 weapon charge
  • Cost: 30 cycles per shot
  • Input: Left mouse button
  • Fires toward cursor
  • No cooldown (spam-friendly)

BLINK: Teleport
  • Requirement: =100 blink charge (fully charged)
  • Cost: 100 cycles (full depletion)
  • Input: B key
  • Distance: 150px forward
  • Can go through walls

TIMING GUIDE
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

TO FIRE WEAPON ONCE:
  1. Click Q              → +25 CPU
  2. Wait 0.5 seconds     → Weapon charge builds
  3. Left-click fire      → Weapon fires
  Total time: ~0.5 seconds


TO CHARGE BLINK:
  1. Click Q 4 times      → +100 CPU
  2. Wait 3 seconds       → Blink builds to 100
  3. Press B              → Blink!
  Total time: ~4 seconds


TO MAINTAIN STEADY FIRE:
  1. Click Q every 1.0 sec → Sustain CPU
  2. Fire whenever weapon ≥ 30
  3. Continuous cycle
  Result: Constant pressure on enemies


TIPS & TRICKS
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

✓ Use 1-5 keys to find your preferred speed

✓ Blink through walls to escape tight situations

✓ Watch the weapon bar - white = ready, gray = charging


TROUBLESHOOTING
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

WEAPON WON'T FIRE:
  → Check weapon bar (needs orange color, ≥30)
  → Have you clicked Q yet to generate CPU?
  → Wait for bar to fill up

CPU NOT GENERATING:
  → Press Q (keyboard) or Right-Click (mouse)
  → Check console for "CPU Generated!" message
  → Should say "CPU: X/100"

BLINK NOT WORKING:
  → Blink bar must be completely full (100)
  → Takes several clicks of Q to charge
  → Press B when bar is white and full

UI NOT VISIBLE:
  → Should be top-left corner of screen
  → Check that game is running (not paused)
  → Restart the scene
